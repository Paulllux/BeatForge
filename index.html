About Pulsonix

Pulsonix, founded by Paul Ulrix, is a developer of virtual instruments and audio plugins for digital audio workstations (DAWs). We aim to empower musicians and producers with innovative tools to craft unique sounds and beats.

BeatForge

BeatForge is our first app, a powerful beat-making plugin designed for seamless integration into your DAW. Create intricate rhythms, load custom samples, and switch song configurations with ease.
